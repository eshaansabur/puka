What is ArisanBD

ArtisanBD is a website for Bangladeshi people for searching jobs with your skills. Whatever you can do, you can get a job easily. Only you need to earn a good skill. Are you not getting a job because you can not run with your papers? You do not need to run with your papers. You can sign up here and make a profile including your skills.

If your skills are limited, you can find a job. Suppose, you are a student of a renowned university and want to teach students. You can sign up here and create an eye-catching profile so that students can know about your education and contact if needed.

Doctors, engineers can also use our website/application. Suppose, you are a renowned doctor and you have a profile in our website. The patients who are suffering, they can contact you. If you are a software developer or an android developer, you can get your first client by joining our website.

So, don't be late. Join us now. Make your first money and change your life forever.
